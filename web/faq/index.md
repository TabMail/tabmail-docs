# Frequently asked questions

## Does TabMail upload my mailbox?

TabMail is designed to keep mailbox data in Thunderbird. The backend primarily handles authentication, quotas, and LLM completions.

## What Thunderbird versions are supported?

TabMail targets **Thunderbird v145**.

## Do I need an account?

Yes. TabMail uses an account for authentication and usage/quota enforcement.

## What’s the hotkey to open chat?

- **Windows/Linux**: **Alt+Ctrl+L**
- **macOS**: **Alt+Command+L**

## Why doesn’t autocomplete work in my compose window?

TabMail currently requires **plaintext composition**. If your identity is set to compose HTML emails, TabMail compose features may not work properly.
